How it works
From couch to parking spot
in three steps

No app download needed. Works in your browser. Your spot waits for you when you arrive.

01

Pick your mall & level

Choose from 12+ malls across Kochi. Select the basement level that's easiest to reach from the entrance you prefer.

02

See live spot status

A real-time grid shows every spot — green means free, red means taken. Updated every 30 seconds via sensor data.

03

Book and pay

Tap your spot, choose 1 hour, 3 hours or full day, and pay securely. Your booking confirmation is your entry pass.

04

Drive straight in

Show your booking reference at the barrier. Your spot is held for 15 minutes after your chosen arrival time.
